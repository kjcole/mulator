Credits for logic gate images

The images used here were found in the Wikipedia article Logic gate and were credited as "By jjbeard - Own Drawing, made in Inkscape 0.43, Public Domain".

There appear to be two standard shape sets: The ANSI distinctive standard shapes, which I learned, and the simpler, rectangular IEC standard shapes. I have included links to the original art for both sets, but will be using the ANSI shapes.

ANSI (IEEE Std 91/91a-1991)

  • AND
  • OR
  • NOT
  • NAND
  • NOR
  • XOR
  • XNOR

IEC (IEEE Std 91/91a-1991; IEC 60617-12 : 1997)

  • AND
  • OR
  • NOT
  • NAND
  • NOR
  • XOR
  • XNOR
